📘 How To Read This Page
📊
Conviction Score
The conviction score (0–100) measures how strongly three independent signal sources agree on the same directional outcome. Flow sentiment (bullish/bearish options order flow) contributes ±15 points. Dealer delta alignment (are dealers forced to buy or sell?) contributes ±10. GEX regime alignment (Long Gamma = price dampening; Short Gamma = amplification) adds ±10. Squeeze/cascade potential adds a bonus 10. Scores ≥75 = High conviction; 50–74 = Moderate; <50 = Low or conflicting signals.
🎯
Best Strategy Selection
The recommended options strategy is generated by an ML model trained on price action, volatility regime, and technical indicators. The model selects from a universe of 10 defined strategies (Bull/Bear Call/Put Spreads, Iron Condors, Butterflies, Straddles, etc.) based on the directional signal and implied volatility environment. Strategies are sized for a defined risk/reward: net credit strategies collect premium upfront; net debit strategies pay premium for directional leverage. Probability of Profit is the model's estimate of the trade reaching max profit at expiry.
⚡
GEX Regime Impact
Long Gamma means dealers are net long gamma — they sell stock when price rises and buy when it falls, dampening volatility and mean-reverting price action. Range-bound strategies (Iron Condors, short straddles) work best. Short Gamma means dealers are net short gamma — they buy when price rises and sell when it falls, amplifying moves. Directional strategies (spreads, debit spreads) work better. The GEX flip level is the price at which the regime switches from long to short gamma — a critical technical level.
🔮
Direction + Magnitude
Direction (BUY / SELL / NEUTRAL) is the ML model's predicted next-period directional bias. For magnitude, use the combination of: net dealer delta (larger absolute $M = more structural buying/selling pressure), distance to gamma wall (moves to the gamma wall are the most likely magnitude), and squeeze potential (gamma squeeze = rapid, accelerating move). A squeeze flag combined with a BUY direction and High Conviction is the strongest signal for a large, fast upside move.
